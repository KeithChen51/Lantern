# Lighthouse Feature Intro Video

## Style Prompt

Use Lighthouse's existing product language: warm paper surface, ink-black editorial typography, restrained amber accents, soft translucent product panels, and a calm operating-system feel. The video should feel like an internal platform briefing for an automotive after-sales service culture program, not a marketing launch. Motion should be deliberate, page-like, and quietly confident.

## Colors

- Paper background: `#F3EFE0`
- Ink foreground: `#2C2C2C`
- Amber accent: `#D97706`
- Deep olive support: `#49634F`
- Soft rose warning/support: `#B65B51`
- Panel white: `#FBF8ED`

## Typography

- Display and body: `"Noto Serif SC", serif`
- Data labels: `"Cascadia Mono", Consolas, monospace`

## What NOT to Do

- Do not turn the video into a generic SaaS promo with neon gradients or dark dashboards.
- Do not overstate Hermit as an autonomous Agent; it is a domain-specific service-culture assistant.
- Do not make Workshop a broad forum; keep it focused on Do and Don't action cards.
- Do not introduce extra product modules beyond Heart, Mirror, Action, Workshop, Hermit, and the admin review surface.
- Do not use decorative gradients or floating blobs that are not part of the current Lighthouse product language.
