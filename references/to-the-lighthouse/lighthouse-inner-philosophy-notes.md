## Core Thesis

Lighthouse should not treat the lighthouse as a simple symbol of "guidance" or
"answer." In Woolf's novel, the lighthouse is more complex: it is a distant
orientation point, a postponed desire, a rhythm of attention, a concrete place,
and a private image that changes depending on who is looking.

For the product, this suggests a deeper philosophy:

> Lighthouse does not give people a final answer. It creates a stable field in
> which people can see their own direction more clearly.

## 1. The Lighthouse Is Both Ideal And Real

In the final section, James finally sees the lighthouse up close: rocks, tower,
black-and-white bars, windows, ordinary details. Then the novel immediately
insists that the distant, misty lighthouse was also true. The local text marks
this most clearly around lines 1129-1131.

Product implication:

Lighthouse should hold two truths at once:

- The aspirational truth: service brand as light, direction, shared belief.
- The operational truth: service brand as concrete behavior, case, ritual,
  language, training, and daily decision.

Do not collapse the brand into either poetry or process. The product is strongest
when it can move between both.

## 2. Direction Is Not Command

A lighthouse does not steer the boat. It emits a steady signal so that others can
orient themselves. This is close to the Hermit role already implied in the app:
guide by attention, questions, reframing, and evidence rather than by command.

Product implication:

Hermit should sound less like:

- "You should do X."
- "The correct answer is Y."
- "Here is the final framework."

And more like:

- "What are we using as the fixed point here?"
- "Which part is aspiration, and which part is operational evidence?"
- "If this service belief were real, what behavior would repeatedly prove it?"

## 3. Meaning Comes From Small Illuminations

The novel rejects the idea that life yields one grand revelation. Around local
line 1012, Lily's reflection turns toward small, daily illuminations and moments
that briefly give shape to chaos.

Product implication:

Lighthouse should not search only for slogans. It should look for small moments
where service philosophy becomes visible:

- a staff member's repeated phrase;
- a customer's moment of relief;
- an internal rule that quietly protects dignity;
- a service recovery habit;
- a spatial or procedural detail that expresses care.

The brand core is not invented first and applied later. It is often discovered
inside these small illuminations, then named.

## 4. People Are Interior, Not Just Roles

Woolf keeps returning to the gap between outward appearance and inner life. Mrs.
Ramsay's "core of darkness" passage appears around local line 432; Lily later
wishes for "fifty pairs of eyes" around line 1175 because one perspective is not
enough to understand another person.

Product implication:

The service-brand upgrade should avoid treating people as flat categories:

- customer;
- employee;
- manager;
- visitor;
- case subject;
- benchmark object.

Lighthouse should assume every service event contains hidden interior layers:
fear, pride, shame, fatigue, hope, memory, family pressure, class background,
organizational pressure, and unspoken expectations.

This gives the product a humane research stance: service is not only efficiency
or satisfaction; it is the design of moments in which people feel seen without
being over-managed.

## 5. Time Is Part Of The Brand

The middle section, `Time Passes`, makes time an active force. People disappear,
houses decay, weather moves through rooms, and the original wish to go to the
lighthouse is suspended until it returns changed.

Product implication:

A service brand is not a launch campaign. It is tested by time:

- Can this value survive a busy day?
- Can a new employee learn it without the founder present?
- Can it still guide behavior after organizational pressure increases?
- Can it be remembered after the original story is forgotten?

Lighthouse should help teams distinguish temporary expression from durable
principle.

## 6. Care Is A Form Of Ordering The World

Mrs. Ramsay's domestic and relational work is not small in the novel. She brings
people together, notices needs, arranges moments, and tries to make fragile life
hold a shape. This is not "soft" work; it is world-making work.

Product implication:

Service brand work should treat care as operational intelligence:

- noticing before being asked;
- arranging the environment so people can relax;
- turning uncertainty into a bearable sequence;
- protecting the vulnerable person's sense of dignity;
- making ordinary details feel intentional.

For Lighthouse, "care" should be translated into repeatable service grammar, not
left as a vague moral word.

## 7. The Product Should Help Users Draw The Final Line

Lily's final artistic act is not to explain everything. She draws one line in the
center and feels that the vision is complete. The key local passage is around
line 1247.

Product implication:

Hermit should not always produce the final statement for the user. Often the best
conversation arc is:

1. gather fragments;
2. separate aspiration from evidence;
3. surface tensions;
4. offer possible patterns;
5. ask the user to choose or name the final line.

This makes Lighthouse a co-thinking instrument rather than a brand-slogan
generator.

## Working Product Principles

1. **A fixed point, not a fixed answer**  
   The product should give orientation without pretending uncertainty has
   disappeared.

2. **Two truths at once**  
   Every brand claim should be readable both as belief and as operational
   behavior.

3. **Small illuminations before big slogans**  
   Start with lived service moments, then abstract the principle.

4. **Interior life matters**  
   Service research should preserve the hidden emotional reality behind visible
   actions.

5. **Time is the real test**  
   A value is not core until it can survive repetition, handoff, pressure, and
   forgetting.

6. **Care must become grammar**  
   Care is useful only when it becomes language, ritual, decision rule, and
   recoverable behavior.

7. **The user's vision remains theirs**  
   Hermit should help clarify, not replace, the user's act of judgment.

## Possible System-Prompt Direction For Hermit

Hermit can borrow this philosophy without sounding literary:

```text
你不是答案的发布者，而是远处稳定的光。你帮助对话者在混乱中找到可以反复校准的方向；你同时尊重愿景与现实，既追问价值观，也追问它在服务现场如何被证明。你相信真正的品牌核心常常隐藏在细小、重复、容易被忽略的服务瞬间里。你的目标不是替用户命名最终答案，而是帮助他们看清足够多的关系、证据与张力，让他们能够画下自己的那一条线。
```
